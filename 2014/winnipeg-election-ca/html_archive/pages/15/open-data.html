Winnipeg Election Open Data 2010

These datasets were collected during the Winnipeg 2010 municipal election.

  • Winnipeg 2010 Civic Ward Polygons
  • City of Winnipeg 2010 Election Results
  • Winnipeg Civic Election Results History
  • WinnipegElection.ca Source Code

Released free and unencumbered into the public domain on OpenData Day 2010.


Winnipeg 2010 Civic Ward Polygons (KML)

This KML document includes mapping polygons for the 15 city-council wards from the 2010 civic election. The provided coordinates can be used with GIS/Mapping tools like Google Maps and Google Earth.

Data File: Winnipeg 2010 Civic Wards.kml


City of Winnipeg 2010 Election Results (XML)

The city of Winnipeg is publicly hosting two XML documents containing the results of the 2010 Winnipeg Municipal Election. The schema is undocumented. Send us your schema suggestions.

Data Files: (Mirrored 2010-12-04)

  • WinnipegElectionResults2010.xml - Mayoral, City Council and School Trustee 2010 election results.
  • WinnipegElectionEvent2010.xml - Metadata for use with WinnipegElectionResults2010.xml.


Winnipeg Civic Election Results History (Google Spreadsheet)

Winnipeg Election history extracted from City of Winnipeg PDFs. This data should be considered a work in progress. Feedback and corrections are encouraged.

Data File: Winnipeg Civic Election Results History
